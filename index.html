About me

This website was created by Varshinee in 2025. Music history is the study of the evolution of music across time, cultures, and geographies. It has been a significant part of human society since prehistoric times. Key events in music history: Prehistoric era The earliest forms of music were probably drum-based, using percussion instruments like rocks and sticks. Renaissance Musicians became more skilled and virtuosic, and it became easier to buy musical scores. Baroque Composers experimented with styles, forms, and instruments, and opera and instrumental music developed. Middle Ages, Renaissance, Baroque, and Classical These are some of the important eras in music history. Notable music history facts The oldest surviving notated work of music is the "Hurrian Hymn to Nikkal", which dates back to around 1400 BCE. Sir John Hawkins wrote the first music history textbook, General History of the Science and Practice of Music, in 1776. Charles Burney was another famous music historian who wrote General History of Music.
